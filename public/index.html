About

FHIRTogether bridges legacy HL7v2 EHR systems and modern FHIR R4 clients, providing a pluggable scheduling gateway with SQLite-backed storage. It supports Schedule, Slot, and Appointment resources along with HL7v2 SIU message translation — making it easy to integrate scheduling into any healthcare workflow.
